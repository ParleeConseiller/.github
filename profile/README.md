## About Us

**Parlee Conseiller, Inc.** is a strategy and technology consulting firm founded in **2002** in Austin, Texas. For over two decades we have served clients across the United States, Canada, Mexico, Argentina, and France with a single conviction: the firms that win are the ones who treat strategy and technology as one discipline, not two.

The name reflects that bilingual heritage — *Conseiller* is French for **Adviser**, the role we have played for our clients since day one.

Today, Parlee Conseiller operates as a **parent holding company**: nine specialized Business Units, each leading its own domain, together forming a complete ecosystem of business and technology solutions — plus **Parlee Nexus**, a growing portfolio of independent ventures across hospitality, wellness, real estate, education, and more.

> Nine specialized divisions. One ecosystem. Decades of trust.
